Our Mission

To be a passionate, trusted, and dedicated team of event professionals committed to providing extraordinarily flawless events that supply our clients with seamless management, timeless style, unforgettable experiences, and the highest quality. To anticipate our clients' needs and deliver exceptional service and products. To meet our clients's event objectives while exceeding their expectations. To provide a great place to work, where our staff is inspired to work to their full potential, and encouraged to find balance in life between their commitments to family, community, and continued education. To remain a premier special event and destination management company while maintaining our mission and values.

Our Values

Integrity: We provide a fair price for the services that we offer with complete transparency. Our clients will always know how their event resources are allocated.
Passion :We don't believe in the status quo our goal is to not meet expectations but to exceed them. Accountability: The Wonder Events's team is personally invested in your event and as your trusted partner we deliver…guaranteed!
Creativity : Wonder Events is a collaborative, full spectrum, energetic, and innovative team.
Quality : We have working relationships with vendors who share our mission and values in all price points.
Professionalism : The Wonder Events's clientele is our top priority and we treat them with the utmost respect and courtesy at all times.
